AndroidCrypt Privacy Policy

This application does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or data from its users.

DATA COLLECTION
We do not collect any data. This includes but is not limited to:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Device information
- Location data
- Usage statistics
- Analytics or telemetry data
- Cookies or tracking identifiers

DATA STORAGE
All encryption operations are performed locally on your device. Your passwords, encryption keys, and encrypted files never leave your device and are never transmitted to any external servers.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
This application does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

NETWORK ACCESS
This application does not require or use internet connectivity. All functionality operates entirely offline on your device.

CONTACT
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer.

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
